My ability in Photography

Photography was no easy hobby to take up. It's incredibly daunting trying to find what equipment is most suitable for a beginner. There is millions of reviews and opinions out there which makes it hard for an indecisive person such as myself. I went with the Nikkon D3300, It was the most recommended camera by the vast majority which now, i completely agree. Another daunting task was trying to find the right camera lens. Again, i ended up going with the most recommended which was the 35mm f 1.8g lens.
